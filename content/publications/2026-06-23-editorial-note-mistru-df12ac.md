### **Final Thought**
*"Eternal vigilance is the price of liberty."* — Often attributed to Thomas Jefferson, this adage remains as relevant today as it was in the 18th century. The erosion of natural rights is not an abstract concern; it is a lived reality for millions of people around the world. It is the duty of every citizen, every institution, and every generation to resist this erosion and to ensure that the flame of liberty continues to burn brightly.
